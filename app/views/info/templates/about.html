About Discovery Bot

Let the Bot Do It.

DiscoveryBot helps attorneys hasten the process of discovering information about the people and property involved in their cases. Data are drawn from many sources, including:

  • Public Data (www.publicdata.com)
  • Zillow (www.zillow.com)
  • DiscoveryBot proprietary algorithms, AI models, and learned heuristics.
